Buran Pass Trek

Shimla district in Himachal Pradesh is an unexplored trekking destination. It is easy to moderate difficulties. Such a trek is a Buran Pass Trek. Buran Pass Trek served as a pastoral way between Pabbar Valley (Shimla district) to Sangla Valley (Kinnaur district). Trek involves the crossing of Buran Ghati (15500 ft climb). This trek is blended of lush green deodar and pine forests.

Pick up and Drop - Chandigarh.

Trek duration - 8 days/7 nights.

Risk Factor - Moderate.

Meals - Breakfast,Lunch,Dinner,Evening Snacks.

Includes - trekking equipments,tents,sleeping bags.
